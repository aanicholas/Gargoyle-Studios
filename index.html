About Me

Instructional design, 3D modeling, Game development

I’m a multidisciplinary digital creator with a background in 3D modeling, visualization, and technical design, and a growing focus on game development and interactive storytelling. My work blends engineering-driven thinking with cinematic, science-fiction–inspired worlds — from architectural visualization and product design to real-time environments and interactive prototypes.

I’m deeply fascinated by advanced propulsion systems, especially the Project ROVER nuclear thermal rocket program, as well as emerging technologies like 3D printing, real-time rendering, and procedural world building. These interests strongly influence both my visual style and the kinds of projects I pursue.

Gargoyle Studios began as a personal creative lab — a place to experiment, learn, and push the limits of what a solo creator can build. Today it represents the foundation of a future independent game studio focused on atmospheric science fiction, simulation-inspired mechanics, and player-driven storytelling.

I’m always learning, building and exploring new tools. Every project here is part of a larger journey toward gaining as much knowledge as I can and learning from pain points along the way.

Highlights

Instructional Design & Multimedia Development

With ~19 years of directly relevant instructional/digital learning experience, I design and build accessible, multimedia-rich training experiences used by worldwide audiences. I specialize in Articulate Storyline, video production, motion graphics, and WCAG-compliant learning design.

3D Modeling & Visualization

I specialize in technical 3D modeling for illustration and real-time engines, architectural visualization, and concept development. I work primarily in Maya and Unreal Engine, with a focus on clean topology, optimized assets, and production-ready pipelines.

Game Development & Interactive Systems

Game development is where all my interests converge — art, engineering, storytelling, and systems design. I’m currently focused on learning and developing in Unreal Engine, building interactive prototypes and gameplay systems.
